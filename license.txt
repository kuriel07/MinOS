WHAT IS IT?

Minos (MIght Not an Operating System, named after the king of crete in greek
mythology) is a realtime operating system design with portability in
mind, user can even switch whether to use cooperative scheduling or 
preemptive scheduling. Because minos was designed with portability in 
mind, it can be ported to several machine. This software is the 
exclusive property of the authors and is covered by this license 
agreement.
